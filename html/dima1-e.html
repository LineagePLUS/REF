There are many notable places in this remote region of Oren.

Glacial Lake, Crystal Cavern, border camp, etc... But the number one symbol of Oren is surely none other than the Ivory Tower, don't you think? Except for the Tower of Oman -- also known as the curse of Aden -- the Ivory Tower is the tallest building in Aden.

Sometimes, just looking at the beautiful tower shooting up into the sky overwhelms me with emotion.

But personally, I find the reflection of the tower on the surface of the Glacial Lake more beautiful than the view of the tower itself reaching high into the sky.
