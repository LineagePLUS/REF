Help: Level 1 Dark Spirit Magic
Blind Hiding:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: 30 seconds

Delay: 10 seconds

Target: Caster

Effect: Renders user invisible. Effect disappears if any active behavior other than movement is performed.

Enchant Venom:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/1/1

Duration: 3 minutes

Delay: None

Target: Caster

Effect: Bestows weapon with venom, making poison attacks possible. One dark stone is required for this magic.

Bring Stone:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 5/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Delay: None

Target: Item

Effect: Changes the nature of a mineral called bring stone by expending one's own mana.
A bring stone can be refined to a total of four levels and has a variety of uses, such as a magic catalyst or as an ingredient in other items.

Bring stone->dark stone->brave stone->wise stone->kaiser stone

As the level increases by one, the success rate falls by half. On the other hand, the higher a character's level and WIS value, the higher the success rate will be.

To use this magic, click on the applicable mineral after casting the bring stone spell. If successful, it will change to a mineral that is one level higher. If it fails, the respective mineral will disappear.

Shadow Armor:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/5

Duration: 15 minutes

Delay: None

Target: Caster

Effect: The user's AC receives a temporary +3 bonus.

Dress Mighty:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/5

Duration: 15 minutes

Delay: None

Target: Caster

Effect: Increases the STR value by 2.
