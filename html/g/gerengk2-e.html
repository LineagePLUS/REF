Gereng:

Those who study the two opposing forces which underlie every phenomenon in our world - Einhasad's benevolence and Gran Kain's malevolence - know much about the gods, but they are also the furthest from those deities.

This place is known to have been a training ground for Wizards, but when Hardin was still active, the Elven Forest or the Ivory Tower of Oren were true centers of magical activity.
