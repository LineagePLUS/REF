Gereng:

There are eight ingredients used in magic spells - crimson mushroom, dragon's teeth, holy blue grapes, mountain ginseng, floating-eye meat, willow root, shard of jade, and bone powder. Each ingredient has different properties and therefore, costs different amounts. Fortunately this area has an abundant supply of floating-eye meat and bone powder, but more valuable ones such as dragon's teeth and crimson mushrooms are hard to come by...
