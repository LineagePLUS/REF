Some spells require a ritual to be effective. Of course, the ritual varies according to the exact spell, but sacrificing something to a god is customary. Sacrifices could be your magical energies, or even your life force. The details depend on the exact spell, so you should search for the ritual after learning those spells.

Also, some spells require a special material called a magic gem in addition to mana. Casting certain spells is impossible without these gems.

It is known that magic such as Curse: Paralyze, Weapon Break, Slow, Create Zombie, Summon Monster, Fog of Sleeping, Create Magical Weapon, Reflecting Pool and Stop must use magic gems.
