Polymorph Magician:
I'm a magician learning the skill of shape changing. For a small contribution of 20 adena, I will cast a spell that will temporarily shape change you into a creature of your choice. The enchantment will only last 30 minutes.

You'll also take on the attributes of the creature you become-becoming faster or stronger depending on type. Choose wisely! Some monsters have disadvantages as well. They may be slower, incapable of wearing armor, or wielding certain weapons.

Pay fee and request shape change.
