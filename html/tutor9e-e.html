Storing Items:

Click on the storage NPC (dwarf or elf). Select "Store Item" under the Personal Storage heading. Scroll through the list to identify the items you wish to store. Highlight the items by clicking on the icon. Type the quantity you want to store, or use the arrows in the list of goods. Click OK.
Your items are now stored. They will no longer show up in your item window/inventory.

Retrieving Items:

Click on the storage NPC (dwarf or elf). Select "Retrieve Item" under the Personal Storage heading. Scroll through the list to identify the items you wish to retrieve. Highlight the items by clicking on the icon. Type the quantity you want to retrieve, or use the arrows in the list of goods. Click OK. (Remember that it will cost your 30 adena or 2 lumps of pure mithril for each type of item retrieved!)
