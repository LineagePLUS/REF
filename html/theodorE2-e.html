If you bring twigs of ent (which you can get by hitting ents with your bare hands) to Nerupa, she can make you bows, arrows, spears, or swords. And they can also be used to make clothes by extracting threads from the arachne. Furthermore, you can neutralize poison by chewing them. Sometimes ents drop fruit, and that has the power to heal Elven bodies. Ents' bodies are helpful to us; such is Einhasad's will. Sometimes I have doubts whether it is right for one life form to exist solely for another life.
