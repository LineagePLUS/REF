Magic dice were developed at the Ivory Tower as a magical tool for fortune telling. There are two kinds of magic dice around now.

There are the four-sided dice showing the directions of north, south, east and west and the six-sided dice that randomly have the numbers one through six.

As a basic prerequiste to using the magic dice, you'll need to have magic stones.

One magic stone is consumed each time you use the dice.
