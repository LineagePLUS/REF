The weapon of an adventurer is very important because it protects his/her life. Of course, for a Wizard, magic may be more useful than a weapon but no matter how great the Wizard is, he/she cannot deal with all enemies using magic alone. So, you can safely say that weapons are the most important tool for everyone.

In order to be equipped with a weapon, double-click the desired item in the item window. It's really very simple. This only works though if you are not already holding another weapon. One thing, there are one-handed weapons and two-handed weapons. Be aware that you cannot use a sheild when holding a two-handed weapon.
