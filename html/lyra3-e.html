Lyra:

The Northern Forest belonged to the orc tribes in the past. Originally there were dozens of small and large tribes, but about ten years ago, one-eyed Seghem, the chief of the Atuba tribe, started conquering other tribes and now 'tis one big tribal community.

He is greatly worried about the fact that their Northern Forest home is disappearing to the humans, and 'tis why there is a constant struggle between humans and orcs in the forest. But the people here are also the victims of the war, it is only the orcs that are gaining benefits from the chaos brought by the war.
