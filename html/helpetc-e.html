Command Help: Miscellaneous
Call Up Help:

Click on the question mark icon to the bottom right of the Chat Window or press Ctrl-H.

Information on User:

From the Chat Window, type '/who [user name]'. If [user name] is not entered, the number of users currently logged on is shown.

Sound On/Off:

If you want to adjust the volume of background music or sound effects, type "/music" or "/sound" in the Chat Window. Then move the volume cursor to adjust the volume of background music or sound effects.

Turning the Movable HP/MP Bar On or Off:

Press Ctrl-R when you want to open or close the moveable HP/MP bar.
