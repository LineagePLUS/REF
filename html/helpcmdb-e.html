Command Help: Blood Pledge
/create [name]

Creates a Blood Pledge. Only Princes or Princesses level 15 or higher may use this command.

/leave

Removes you from your current Blood Pledge.

/join

Allows you to join a Blood Pledge. You must be facing the leader of the Blood Pledge to do so.

/title [name] [title]

Assigns a title to a Blood Pledge member. Only Princes or Princesses may use this command to assign titles to their Blood Pledge members.

/emblem [filename]

May be used by Blood Pledge leaders to select a file name as the emblem for a Blood Pledge. Emblem files should be located in your Lineage directory.

/pledge

Displays information about your Blood Pledge

/war

Declares war on a Blood Pledge. May be used only by Blood Pledge leaders.

/surrender

Surrenders a Blood Pledge war. May be used only by Blood Pledge leaders.

/cease

Declares a ceasefire in a Blood Pledge war. May be used only by Blood Pledge leaders.

/alliance [pledge name]

Used by Blood Pledge leaders to declare an alliance with another Blood Pledge.

/ban [name]

Removes a member from a Blood Pledge. May be used only by Blood Pledge leaders.

/checkbmp [filename]

May be used by Blood Pledge leaders to check an emblem file for the game. The file should be in .bmp format, 16w x 12h pixels.
