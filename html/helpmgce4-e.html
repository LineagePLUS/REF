Help: Level 4 Spirit Magic - Earth
Earth Bind:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Ingredients: 2 spirit gems

Duration: 10 seconds

Target: 1 PC

Effect: Temporarily prevents attacks -- either by or upon the target -- for the duration.

Bless of Earth:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 40/0

Ingredients: None

Duration: 30 minutes

Target: Party to which the caster belongs

Effect: Bonus of 5 is given to the AC of all party members for the duration.
