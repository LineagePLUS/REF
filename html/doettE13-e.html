Doett:

It's a metal produced by refining mithril. It is also called "God's metal" - once you make a weapon with this, the weapon is more powerful than one made with mithril. However, the refining method is only known to the Fairy Queen. If you want to see her, you should check the flower bed on the way to the Lawful Temple located northwest of town. Fairies can be quite capricious, so be careful once you find them!


Oh - also, oriharukons can also be used to fix eroded weapons and armors. In fact, that's the best way to fix armor eroded by a blob.
