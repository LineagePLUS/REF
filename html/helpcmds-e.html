Command Help: Chatline
/Fa

Lists all shortcut keys (F1- F9) and items and magic spells assigned to each.

/F5-F8 [item name]

Assigns a specific item to a shortcut key. For example, '/F5 healing potion' would assign a healing potion to your F5 key.

/F5-F8 [spell name]

Assigns a specific spell to a shortcut key.

/who

Reports the total number of users currently playing on the server.

/who [name]

Displays the online status of a character where [name] is the character's name.

/loc

Displays your current location.

/chat [off/on]

Toggles the Chat Window open and closed.

/whisper [off/on]

Toggles whisper on and off

/obscene [off/on]

Toggles the obscenity filter.

/sound [off/on]

Toggles the game sound effects on and off.

/music [off/on]

Toggles game music on and off.

/bubble

Toggles speech bubbles on and off.

/lang [english/korean/japanese/chinese]

Selects a game client language.

/clientonly

Allows you to take a screenshot that does not include the chat window and status bar.

/ver

Displays the current client version.

/uptime

Displays the current date and the elapsed time since the last server reset.

/trade

Opens a Trade Window with the player you are currently facing.
