Narhen:

They are the race with a human torso and the legs of a goat. I heard that Einhasad created them from a goat grazing the grasses around the Mother Tree.
It's hard to get close to them, because they are straightforward and violent, but they are the most courageous among the guardians and thus contribute the most in protecting the forest. Further you can get useful materials like pan's mane from them.
