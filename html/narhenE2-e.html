Narhen:

Pans are famous for their ferocity, because they try to trample everything that stands in their way. Nothing except music can restrain them, because of its purity.
But we Elves can make useful things by taking advantage of that. If you bring ecdysis along with mithril or oriharukon, they will make a mithril plate or an oriharukon plate by trampling the materials with their hard hooves.
The ones most afraid of their ferocity are the humans who want to invade the forest; pans are the most trustworthy guardians of the forest.
