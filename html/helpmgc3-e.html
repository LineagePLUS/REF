Help: Third Level Spells
Lightning:

Type: Attack, Air

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC within 6 cells of the caster

Effect: Shoots lightning from the user's weapon, damaging the target and all enemies along a straight line between the user and target.

Turn Undead:

Type: Life, Earth

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One undead NPC

Effect: Has a certain probability of killing an undead. Has a 5% chance of inducing a berserker rage within an undead.

Heal:

Type: Heal, Earth

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Restores target's HP moderately, or can be used to inflict damage upon an undead.

Curse - Blind:

Type: Curse

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: 10 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Has a blinding effect on the target.

Blessed Armor:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: 30 minutes

Target: One piece of body armor carried by the caster

Effect: Reduces the target armor's AC by 3.

Frozen Cloud:

Type: Attack, Water

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Conjures up freezing air around the target causing damage.

Reveal Weakness:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 8/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Magically reveals the type of energy around the target in order to disclose which element would cause the greatest damage.
