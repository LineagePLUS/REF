Bernard the Trainer:

It's always wise to know about about your armor and weapons, and I'm happy to tell you!

To equip a weapon, just double-click it in your inventory. When you move your cursor over the weapon it will tell you whether its held or not.
Equipping armor works the same - but take note! Just like getting dressed in the morning, you must put your armor on in order. You can't put on your armor after you put on your cloak, for example.
Certain types of armor may not be available to you. You can get an idea of what items you can wear by visiting the merchant in town. Click on the items in his inventory and if they are only available to certain classes, you'll see those classes listed.
Good luck to you!
