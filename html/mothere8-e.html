Mother of the Forest and Elves:

You didn't forget that every being in this forest is fundamentally self-sufficient, did you? The rule around here is that you should get and make everything you need on your own.

Well, I say that, but... Nerupa can help you make almost everything you need if you bring the materials to her. And you can get the materials from guardians or at various places in the forest. Ask some of the Elves in the town and they will give you a lot of good advice.
