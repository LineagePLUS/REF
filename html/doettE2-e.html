We Elves lose our power once we touch normal metal...so we can't approach iron or copper - they are metals.
Mithril, however, is different - it's blessed by Einhasad Herself, so the essence of fairy is still there. Further it's very light, and strong - quite suitable for making weapons.
How do you make mithril? The way to refine rough mithril into pure mithril is known only to the fairy folk. If you can bring rough mithril to them, they will make it pure.
