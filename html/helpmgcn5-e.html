Help: Level 5 - Common Line of Spirit Magic
Area of Silence:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 80/0

Ingredients: 10 spirit gems

Duration: Maximum of 10 seconds

Target: Screen

Effect: Temporarily inhibits use of magic by everyone on the screen of the caster.

Summon Greater Elemental:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 80/0

Ingredients: 2 spirit gems

Duration: 1 hour

Target: None

Effect: Summons the powerful spirit of a chosen line of magic. Other existing spirits cannot be called while this spell is in effect. Can be used only after choosing a line of magic through Ellyonne at the Ivory Tower of Oren.
