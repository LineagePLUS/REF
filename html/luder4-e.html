Guardian Luder:

This is the Singing Island and it can be said that it is the "twin" of the Talking Island.

There are various facilities set up on the Talking Island for the many novice adventurers, such as a training camp. But there are still enough dangers lurking about to threaten the life of a beginner adventurer. So, Lord Gunter has prepared this place for the safety of rank beginners so that they can take various kinds of training in peace.

There are no murderers or fierce monsters that you are incapable of dealing with, nor are there experienced adventurers who do not need the training offered on this island. All you need to do is be at peace in your mind, sharpen and polish your sword, and learn with friends of similar ability. Ha, ha, ha!

But do remember one fact. Someday you will become an able adventurer who no longer needs the training offered on this island and must leave...
