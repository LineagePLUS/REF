Seven Commandments from Heaven
1. Thy name signifies thy face; thou shalt live on with a good name. 2. Thou shalt not trouble thy neighbor with aggressive and mean words. 3. The wealth thou hast accumulated in Aden shall be traded only in Aden territory. 4. The land of Aden is a place for rest and thou shalt adjust thy time to take breaks. 5. Thou shalt always think of thy neighbor before thyself. 6. Thou shalt never commit the crime of coveting another's wealth and stealing from thy neighbor. 7. Thou shalt prefer just and fair game above false victory.
Follow the words of these commandments.
