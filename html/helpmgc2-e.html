Help: Second Level Spells
Cure Poison:

Type: Life

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC

Effect: Cures poisoning.

Chill Touch:

Type: Death, Water

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 10/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC adjacent to the caster

Effect: Takes a small portion of HP from the target and gives it to the user.

Curse - Poison:

Type: Curse, Water

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 10/10

Ingredients: 1 magic gem

Duration: 30 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC, Contact

Effect: Poisons the target. Causes target's HP to continuously fall for one minute.

Enchant Weapon:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: One weapon of the user

Effect: Raises the target's experience value by 2.

Detection:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Current screen

Effect: Locates hidden PCs or NPCs on the screen of the user. Reveals PCs wearing cloaks of invisibility or hidden spartoi, stone golems and other hidden creatures.

Decrease Weight:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/50

Duration: 30 minutes

Target: Caster

Effect: Reduces the weight of items so that more than usual can be carried around.

Fire Arrow:

Type: Attack, Fire

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 8/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: A flaming magical arrow.

Stalac:

Type: Attack, Earth

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 8/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: A stalactite appears beneath the target causing damage.
