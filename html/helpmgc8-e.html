Help: Eighth Level Spells
Full Heal:

Type: Heal, Earth

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 48/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Restores target's HP significantly. Inflicts damage on the undead.

Firewall:

Type: Attack, Fire

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 60/0

Duration: 10 seconds

Target: Area on the screen

Effect: Creates a wall of fire in the target area. All PCs and NPCs coming into contact with the firewall are damaged at a set rate.

Blizzard:

Type: Attack, Water

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 60/10

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: All enemies on the screen

Effect: Inflicts damage on all visible enemies with a very cold wind.

Invisibility:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: Until MP is used up

Target: Caster

Effect: Hides the user from other PCs or NPCs. Any actions other than movement will cancel the spell.

Resurrection:

Type: Life

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 50/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One dead PC or NPC

Effect: Resurrects the target.

Earthquake:

Type: Attack, Earth

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 40/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: NPC within 3 cells of caster

Effect: Generates an earthquake around the user, damaging the target.

Life Stream:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 32/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 5 minutes

Target: A certain area on the screen

Effect: Creates an energy of life field in the target area. Characters within range have their natural HP recovery amount increased.

Silence:

Types: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 10 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Restricts PC's and NPC's use of magic in a narrow area.
