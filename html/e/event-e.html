Summer Event!

7/1 - 8/31, Lineage Player Appreciation Event!
This event is a bonus occasion provided to show our appreciation for all Lineage users.

"Enjoy Lineage! Get the Bonus!"
T-shirts are just the beginning, as we'll also be giving away 50 Microsoft wheel mice and two Compaq notebook computers every week (for a total of 12) -- and every month, a game PC that every gamer dreams about!

Participate now. The choice is clear.

Bonus 1
Everyone who joins Lineage as a full-subscription player during the event period will receive a specially-made Lineage collection t-shirt.

Bonus 2
Everyone who joins as a full-subscription player for at least three months during the event period will receive a Lineage jigsaw puzzle (1000 pieces). Of course, you'll still get the Lineage collection t-shirt.

Bonus 3
Every week, 50 people who have joined as regular players during the event period will be selected by lottery to receive a Microsoft Wheel Mouse.

Bonus 4
Every week during the event, the male and female character in a particular class with the highest level will be determined and each will receive a Compaq notebook computer -- for a total of 12 computers during the entire event. Each week (except for the last week), a character class (Prince/Princess, Knight, Elf or Wizard) will be chosen and the highest level male and female character in that class will each be awarded a notebook computer (Compaq Armada 15000C).

Bonus 5

In the last week of each month, the top Blood Pledge will be determined and the lord of that pledge will be awarded what every game maniac longs for -- a Fantasy Game PC.

Attention!! Announcement of Event Details
- We will announce event news in the public notices of the Lineage game every day during the duration of the event.
- A list of the winners will be posted in the public notices of the game and in the event details page (http:// lineage.ncsoft.co.kr/lineage_new/event/) every Friday and Saturday.


Rules of the Game for Top Character and Blood Pledge
- Anyone who joins as a regular user during the event is eligible.

- Criteria for Determining Top Character: Characters created on the designated server between Monday at midnight and Friday at 8:00pm each week.
*The designated server and selected character will be announced on the notice board of the game every week.

- Criteria for Determining Best Blood Pledge: No limitation on the date of character creation. However, all members of the Blood Pledge must be full-subscription players.

- Any participant who PKed even once during the event loses eligibility to be selected as the top character or Blood Pledge.
Therefore, a Blood Pledge with a member who PKed also loses eligibility.

- Selection of best character or Blood Pledge is limited to one time per account.
And selection of best Blood Pledge is also limited to one time.
