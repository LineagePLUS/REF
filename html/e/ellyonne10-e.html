Spirit magic, as the name suggests, is magic that takes its energy from the spirits that embody the fundamental energies of this world.

The reason humans cannot use this magic is that people are, unlike Elves, nowhere near forming a comradeship with the spirits.

Spirit magic can be classified into five lines.

They are: the Common Line, the Line of Fire, the Line of Water, the Line of Wind and, lastly, the Line of Earth.
