Magician Siriss:

Simply put, magic can be summed up as the acts of big and small miracles achieved by borrowing the power of the gods. Divided roughly, there are two categories: the white magic of the good god Einhasad, and the black magic of the evil god Gran Kain. Now, that is really simplifying the issue enormously, so if you want to learn in more detail about these complex magical theories, you had better ask Sir Gereng of the Talking Island. In any case, if you are a Knight who bets everything on the sword or are a person who does not acknowledge the existence of the gods, you can neither learn nor use magic.
