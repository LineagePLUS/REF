Help: Level 1 - Common Spirit Magic
Resist Magic:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/0

Ingredients: 1 spirit gem

Duration: 1 hour

Target: Caster

Effect: The user's MR is temporarily increased.

Body to Mind:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 0/8

Ingredients: None

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Caster

Effect: The caster's HP is decreased and MP is recovered at the moment the user casts this magic.

Teleport to Mother:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/0

Ingredients: None

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Caster

Effect: Unless the user is standing in an area that prohibits teleportation, the user will be teleported to the Mother Tree in the Elven Forest.
