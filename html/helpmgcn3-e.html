Help: Level 3 - Common Line of Spirit Magic
Return to Nature:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 50/0

Ingredients: 1 spirit gem

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One magically summoned or tamed monster

Effect: Releases the summoning or taming of a monster which has been summoned or tamed through a magical method. Consumes one spirit gem.

Blood to Soul:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 0/40

Ingredients: None

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Caster

Effect: Consumes some of the caster's HP and recovers some MP.

Protection from Elemental:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 40/0

Ingredients: None

Duration: 1 hour

Target: Caster

Effect: The caster's resistance to elemental magic of his own type is increased for the duration.
