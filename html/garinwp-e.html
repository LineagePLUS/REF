Storage House Keeper Garin:

Setting the Storage House Password
 1. Click "Set/Change Password".
2. Click "OK".
3. Enter a new password and click "OK". (Must be a six-digit number.)
4. Enter the new password again and click "OK" to confirm.
5. The storage house password has been set.

Using the Storage House
 1. Click "Retrieve Item".
2. Enter the six-digit password and click "OK".
3. Get your item(s) from the storage house.
4. The storage house password also works at the Blood Pledge storage house.

Changing the Storage House Password
 1. Click "Set/Change Password".
2. Enter the current password and click "OK".
3. Enter a new password and click "OK". (Must be a six-digit number.)
4. Enter the new password again and click "OK" to confirm.
5. The storage house password has been changed.

Removing the Storage House Password
 1. Click "Set/Change Password".
2. Enter the current password and click "OK".
3. Click "OK" without entering anything into the change password window.
4. Click "OK" without entering anything into the confirm password window.
5. The storage house password has been removed.

Special Notes
 1. The storage house password must be a six-digit number.
2. The storage house password can be set at Game Options.
