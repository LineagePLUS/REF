Horun:

To learn 1st level spells, bring me 10 arachne's webs, 50 lumps of pure mithril, 100 pieces of fairy dust, or 10 pan's manes.

To learn 2nd level spells, bring me 10 mithril threads, 8 fungus juices, 1 pan's horn, or 3 barks of ent.

To learn 3rd level spells, bring me 45 oriharukon, 3 ecdysis of arachne, 3 alae of fairy, or 3 pan's horns.
