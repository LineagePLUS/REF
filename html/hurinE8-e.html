Hurindalien:

She is the elder of arachnes and seems to be an old acquaintance of the Mother of the Forest and Elves. She was living in the cave under the Mother Tree even before I was born...
She has great talent for making items; in fact she makes a variety of things for us. Usually arachnes are cold, but polite...but Nerupa is, well...cranky. Don't expect to hear pleasant words from her, but don't let that stop you from showing gratitude for her help.
