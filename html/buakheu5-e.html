As soon as the magical boundary of water disappeared, Fafurion began its attack on Eva's Kingdom with foul and polluted water. The beautiful temples and houses fell; light in the colors of the rainbow no longer shone throughout the city.

Goddess Eva and the people of the water city fought with all their might and succeeded in sealing Fafurion in a cave on the outskirts of the city, but the Fafurion's blood flowed from the wound that Eva's Trident inflicted and spread out into the water. All of the underwater people and living creatures polluted with the blood's poison began turning into monsters. It was Fafurion's last curse.

Eva had no other choice but to gather the remaining water people and leave the kingdom. She departed for the surface and now only this wasteland remains.

...this is where my story ends
