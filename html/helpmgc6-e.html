Help: Sixth Level Spells
Create Zombie:

Type: Death, Water

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 5/80/1

Duration: 1 hour

Target: NPC corpse

Effect: Makes a corpse into a zombie. The zombie's abilities are strengthened depending on the caster's level and the zombie created will follow the caster's orders.

Enchant Mighty

Type: Bestow, Fire

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 40/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Raises the target's STR by 5.

Haste:

Type: Control, Wind

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: Caster, one member of the caster's Blood Pledge or one NPC

Effect: Increases the target's movement speed by 1.5 times during the duration.

Cancel Magic:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 40/0

Ingredients: 2 magic gems

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Removes all magic currently influencing the target. If cast 100% to a party or Blood Pledge member, there is a possibility of failure due to the effects of MR from other users.

Eruption:

Type: Attack, Earth

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 32/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Causes a directional earthquake that damages the target.

Sunburst:

Type: Attack, Fire

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 32/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Can be cast at a short distance, causing strong explosive damage to the target.

Weakness:

Type: Curse

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 30 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Weakens target PC or NPC's accuracy by 1 and attack strength by 5.

Bless Weapon:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Duration: 30 minutes

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Blesses the target's weapon, increasing accuracy and attack strength.
