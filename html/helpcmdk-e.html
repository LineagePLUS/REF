Command Help: Keyboard
Control+T:

Changes the size of the chat window.

Control+X:

Sets your last cast spell as a readied spell. This spell will be automatically cast when you click on a target.

Control+P

Captures a screenshot. Screenshots are stored as .tga files in your Lineage directory.

F4

Automatically picks up items from the ground around you.

Control+S

Opens your spellbook.

Tab

Opens your Item Window.

Control+A

Opens your status window.

Control+Q

Opens the Quit/Restart menu.
