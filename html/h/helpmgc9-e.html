Help: Ninth Level Spells
Lightning Storm:

Type: Attack

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 52/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Enemies within five cells of the caster

Effect: Sends down a strong lightning bolt to hit all enemies in a wide range within five cells of the target.

Fog of Sleeping:

Type: Curse, Water

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 30/5

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 30 seconds

Target: Target cell on the screen

Effect: Has a certain probability of inducing sleep on PCs and NPCs within three cells of the target. PCs and NPCs that are sleeping will awaken if attacked.

Polymorph:

Type: Transform, Curse

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 80/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 2 hours

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Has a certain probability of changing the target into a monster of the user's choosing.

Immune to Harm:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Ingredients: 2 magic gems

Duration: 1 minute

Target: Caster or a PC belonging to the caster's Blood Pledge

Effect: Creates a strong magic barrier for the target, blocking 50% of normal damage.

Mass Teleport:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 80/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: User and members of the user's Blood Pledge and monsters that the user has tamed within 1 cell of the user.

Effect: Teleports all targets. Restrictions regarding points of teleportation are the same as "Teleport" spell.

Fire Storm:

Type: Attack

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 52/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Summons a fire storm around the user.

Decay Potion:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Ingredients: 2 magic gems

Duration: 10 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Blocks use of potions by target.

Counter Detection:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Duration: 30/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Target on the screen

Effect: Damages invisible enemy and cancels the invisibility.
