Help: First Level Spells
Lesser Heal:

Type: Earth, Heal

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 4/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Restores target's HP slightly, and inflicts damage on the undead.

Light:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 4/0

Duration: 2 hours

Target: Caster

Effect: A light source brighter than a lantern.

Shield:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 3/5

Duration: 30 minutes

Target: Caster

Effect: Lowers the AC of the target by 1.

Energy Bolt:

Type: Attack, Beam

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 3/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Pure magical energy inflicting minor damage.

Teleport:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Caster

Effect: Teleports user to a random location. If used while wearing the "Ring of Teleport Control" destination can be chosen.

Ice Dagger:

Type: Attack, Water

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 4/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: A small, flying dagger of ice.

Wind Shuriken:

Type: Attack, Air

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 4/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Creates a round-toothed, flying wheel above the head of the user.

Holy Weapon:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Bestows holy power to a weapon. Causes extreme damage to the undead.
