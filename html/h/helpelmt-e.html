The Four Elements
The following elements exist in the Lineage world:

Fire
Fire is stronger than earth.
Fire is weaker than water.

Water
Water is stronger than fire.
Water is weaker than wind.

Wind
Wind is stronger than water.
Wind is weaker than earth.

Earth
Earth is stronger than wind.
Earth is weaker than fire.

Monsters may practice up to level 3 elemental magic. [Reveal Weakness].


[Other types]

Undead
Undead are vulnerable to weapons made of silver, mithril and oriharukon.

Demons
Demons are vulnerable to weapons made of mithril and oriharukon.
