Help: Level 4 Spirit Magic - Water
Nature's Touch:

Type: Heal

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Ingredients: None

Duration: 5 minutes

Target: 1 PC

Effect: Increases the target's ability to recover HP by up to +15. The HP recovery rate formula is "level - 9" and so the maximum effectiveness can be seen when the PC's level is 24 or above.
