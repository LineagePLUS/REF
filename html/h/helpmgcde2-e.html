Help: Level 2 Dark Spirit Magic
Moving Acceleration:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 10/20

Duration: 30 minutes

Delay: None

Target: Caster

Effect: Temporarily increases movement speed of the user by 1.5. Can be used with Haste.

Burning Spirit:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/5

Duration: 5 minutes

Delay: 3 seconds

Target: Caster

Effect: Increases concentration and has a probability of increasing attack power by 1.5.

Dark Blind:

Type: Curse

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: 1 minute

Delay: 1 second

Target: Caster, PC, NPC

Effect: Causes temporary blindness in the target. 100 points are subtracted from alignment every time the spell is cast.

Venom Resist:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/40

Duration: 10 minutes

Delay: None

Target: Caster

Effect: Bestows temporary resistance to poison to the user.

Dress Dexterity:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/5

Duration: 15 minutes

Delay: None

Target: Caster

Effect: Increases DEX value by 2.
