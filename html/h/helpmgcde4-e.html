Help: Dark Elf Magic - Miscellaneous
Magic Window:

Press the Ctrl-S key to bring up the magic window at any time.


Teleport Location Bookmarking:

You can use the scroll of teleportation and a ring of teleport control to teleport to a place you have been before.

Remember your present location by entering "/bookmark [name of location]" into the chatline. The next time you use a teleport scroll, while equipped with the ring of teleport control, this bookmark list will appear. Double-click the place of your choice and you can teleport to that location immediately.

If you just type "/bookmark", you will see the list of locations which you have bookmarked.

To remove a bookmarked location, choose the location to delete from the list and press the Delete key.


Magic Shortcut Keys:

Just as with items, you can assign different magic spells to the F5 - F12 keys. To make a shortcut, enter "/F(5-12) [Name of magic spell]" in the chatline. Another method of making a shortcut is to open the magic window and drag the magic icon for the appropriate spell to the respective slot in the shortcut key window at the lower right-hand corner of the screen.


Switching to Spell Mode:

You can switch to spell mode by pressing Ctrl-X. While in spell mode, you will see the last spell you used appear in the lower right-hand corner of the screen. You can automatically cast a spell by simply clicking on a target. (Keep in mind though, that when starting the game, you won't have used magic yet and so there won't be any magic in memory.) If you press Ctrl-X again, spell mode ends and you can resume normal attacks.


Forced Magic Attack:

If you are not in spell mode, you can still cast spells on a PC by pressing the Ctrl key.
