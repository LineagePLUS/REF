Command Help: Party

It's possible to organize a party of up to eight players to share experiences. Party members can see each other's HP and chat to each other, as well as share experience from battles.

Creating a party:

Type '/invite' while standing in front of the person with whom you would like to form the party; this will create the new party with yourself as the leader.

Inviting new members to the party:

Type '/invite', standing in front of the person you want to invite. This command can only be used by the leader of the party.

Banning party members:

The party leader can ban members from his party. Type '/banparty [character name]' to do this.

Leaving the party:

Type '/outparty' to leave your party. This is necessary before joining a new party. Restarting or quitting will also cause you to leave your party.
