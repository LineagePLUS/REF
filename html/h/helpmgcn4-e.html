Help: Level 4 - Common Line of Spirit Magic
Erase Magic:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Ingredients: 2 spirit gems

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: 1 PC

Effect: Nullifies all lasting magical effects on the target.

Summon Lesser Elemental:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/0

Ingredients: 2 spirit gems

Duration: 1 hour

Target: None

Effect: Summons the spirit of a chosen line of magic. However, during the duration, other existing spirits cannot be called. This magic can be used only after choosing a line of magic through Ellyonne at the Ivory Tower of Oren.
