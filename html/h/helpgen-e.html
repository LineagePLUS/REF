Command Help: General
Walk:

To walk, use the arrow keys on the keyboard or click on your destination on the screen with the mouse.

Change Selection:

When items or characters overlap and the cursor is selecting one object, right-click to change the selected object.

Turn:

To change the direction of your character without moving, click on the desired direction while pressing the Shift key.

Using the Menu Icon Bar:

There are seven menu icon bars at the very bottom on the right side of the screen. You can use the following functions by clicking on each of the icons:


Help Window: Click the question mark-shaped icon or press Ctrl-H.

Status Indicator Window: Click the person-shaped icon or press Ctrl-A.

Magic Window: Click the star-shaped icon or press Ctrl-S.

Item Window: Click the pouch-shaped icon or press the Tab key.

Community Window: Click the icon of two people shaking hands or press the Ctrl-C key.

Option Window: Click the tool-shaped icon or press the Ctrl-V key.

System Window: Click the door-shaped icon or press Ctrl-Q or Alt-X.

Using the Shortcut Key Window:

There are eight button windows above the icon bar at the bottom-right of the screen. Each slot is linked with one of the F5 - F12 keys and can be used if an item or magic icon is designated for the respective empty slot.

The slots can be designated by opening the Item Window or Magic Window and dragging the respective item or magic icon to the desired slot while holding the left mouse button* down, moving the icon to the correct slot and then releasing the mouse button.

To use the item or magic designated in a slot, press the appropriate F5 - F12 key. If you have a wheel mouse, roll the wheel to move the focus and then press the wheel button to use the item or magic for the slot which is in focus. Also, after moving the mouse cursor to a slot, you can use the item or magic by double-clicking the left mouse button.

You can use the eight shortcut keys of F5 - F12. Change to another shortcut key set of eight assignable shortcuts by pressing F1 - F3. You can use the F1 - F3 keys to change between a total of 24 spells and items depending on use.
*Unless otherwise indicated, use the left mouse button if you are playing Lineage on a PC.
