Help: Special Prince Magic
Glowing Aura:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Consumed: 40/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: All party members

Effect: Increases accuracy of targets by 5 and magic defensive strength by 20% for the duration. Can be learned at level 40. This magic can be used cumulatively with other magic having the same abilities.

Brave Aura:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Consumed: 40/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: All party members

Effect: Increases attack ability of the targets by 5 for the duration. Can be learned at level 50. This magic can be used cumulatively with other magic having the same abilities.

Shining Aura:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Consumed: 40/0

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: All Blood Pledge members or party members

Effect: Increases AC of the targets by 8 for the duration. Can be learned at level 55. This magic can be used cumulatively with other magic having the same abilities.
