Help: Fourth Level Spells
Fireball:

Type: Attack, Fire

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Area within 6 cells of the caster

Effect: Hurls a fireball at the target location, inflicting damage on all enemies within three cells from the center.

Physical Enchant-DEX:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 50/0

Duration: 20 minutes

Target: Caster or one member of the Blood Pledge of the caster

Effect: Raises the target's Dexterity by 5. The effect is not cumulative.

Weapon Break:

Type: Transform, Fire

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 32/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One adjacent PC or NPC

Effect: Has a certain probability of eroding a target's weapon. Severity of damage increases with the user's spell level.

Vampiric Touch:

Type: Death, Earth

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC within 3 cells from caster

Effect: Drains HP from the target and adds it to the caster's total.

Slow:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 30 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Slows the target's speed.

Earth Jail:

Type: Attack, Earth

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 24/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: Area within six cells of the caster

Effect: Large thorny brambles shoot up from the ground, surrounding enemies within two cells of the target and then disappear back into the earth, damaging the target.

Counter Magic:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 10 seconds

Target: Caster

Effect: Protects caster from magic attack. Can be used only once.

Meditation:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 5/30

Duration: 10 minutes

Target: Caster

Effect: Can recover MP at a faster rate than normal. Any activity, such as movement or fighting, cancels the effect.
