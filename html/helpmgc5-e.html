Help: Fifth Level Spells
Curse-Paralyze:

Type: Curse, Earth

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 80/0

Ingredients: 2 magic gems

Duration: 2 minutes

Target: One adjacent PC or NPC

Effect: Has a certain probability of paralyzing a target. The duration increases with the user's magic power.

Call Lightning:

Type: Attack, Air

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 24/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC within 6 cells from the caster

Effect: Calls lightning from the sky, inflicting severe damage on the target.

Greater Heal:

Type: Heal, Earth

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Restores target's HP considerably. Also damages undead.

Tame Monster:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 30/20

Ingredients: 1 magic gem

Duration: 1 hour

Target: One monster NPC

Effect: Has a certain probability of charming the target and making it follow the user's orders. The probability of success increases as the target's HP decreases. Some monsters are immune to this spell.

Remove Curse:

Type: Life, Air

Alignment: Lawful

MP/HP Requirement: 20/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC

Effect: Has a certain probability of removing all curses from the target.

Cone of Cold:

Type: Attack, Water

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 24/0

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: An undulating circle of cold air, sent forth to damage the target.

Mana Drain:

Type: Transform

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 1/50

Duration: Instantaneous

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Causes the target PC or NPC's MP to be exhausted and part of it to be absorbed as MP by the user.

Darkness:

Type: Control

Alignment: Chaotic

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Ingredient: 1 magic gem

Duration: 30 seconds

Target: One PC or NPC

Effect: Renders the target blind. Can be healed with Cancel Magic.
