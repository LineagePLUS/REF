Fettered Soul:

All life gets returned to a handful of dust. Such is life…

After you gain this understanding, it will be too late. There is no use for regrets.

Driven by desire, a momentary lapse of reason… A life that cannot return to dust.

It is too late to absolve my sins. Everything in this world means nothing to me now.

I don't know the difference between what is good and what is bad any more.

Only the path to the world beyond will complete the long journey of my harsh and difficult life.



Listen to the whisper.
