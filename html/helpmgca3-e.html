Help: Level 3 Spirit Magic - Wind
Wind Shot:

Type: Bestow

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Ingredients: None

Duration: 15 minutes

Target: One PC

Effect: Increases accuracy of target's long distance attacks by 6.

Wind Walk:

Type: Control

Alignment: Neutral

MP/HP Requirement: 15/0

Ingredients: None

Duration: 30 minutes

Target: Caster

Effect: Increases caster's movement speed. Can be used along with haste magic but not with wafer effects.
