The first thing you should do is equip your weapon and armor.

To do this, open your inventory by pressing the Tab key or click the bag icon in the Menu bar to view your inventory.
Find the icon for the weapon you wish to arm yourself with.
Double-click the icon. Note that it will indicate that the weapon is armed when you put your cursor over the icon in the inventory.
If you double-click the icon again, you will disarm. Use these steps to put on or take off your armor, as well.
