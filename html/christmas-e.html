Let's celebrate the birth date of Einhasad!

Though it's not specified in the Libram, I heard that these red socks were given to the first human by Einhasad, so that she would not hurt her feet on the rough ground.

So to commemorate that event, we have a custom to exchange socks with neighbors. In this way, we hope that the blessing Einhasad gave to the first human may fall upon us as well!

Who knows? If you perform good deeds until December 25, Einhasad Herself may give you a great reward!
