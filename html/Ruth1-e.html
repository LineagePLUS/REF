Would you like to know about armor?

Go to Shari, the leather craftsman who lives in the northern part of Hidden Valley. You should take her seven furs--hunt deer or kobolds for fur.
When you have the furs, ask to Shari make you a leather shield.
Once you've equipped the shield, look at your Armor Class on the lower left of your screen. Did it go down by one number? Remember, a lower AC is better.

Alternatively, you may receive weapons or armor from monsters that you kill which are better than the armor or weaponry you currently possess.
