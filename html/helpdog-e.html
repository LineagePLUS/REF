Animal Tournament is the game in which your animal will fight another's animal.

A player registers his animal and waits until a challenger arrives... The two attendees bet the same money, and the winner takes all.

Giving items or casting spells before the game is alright, but you'd better not use really expensive items if the price will exceed the winning prize.

...one man from Oren said that the fights were disgusting, and asked how could we make our pets fight against each other.

Bah, he doesn't understand we just do this for amusement and some petty cash while avoiding our noble mistress...
