Ruba:

The underground dungeon is a very dangerous place.

If you'll go and explore it for me, I will give you one of my precious possessions as a token of appreciation. But you must bring back a black key from the underground dungeon as proof of your trip. You will be able to find the black key by reading the mysterious writings on the walls down there.

You'll need the help of Wizard Kun to enter the underground dungeon. Wizard Kun is always at the gates, so you will be able to find him easily.
