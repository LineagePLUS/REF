Dekard:

Welcome to the Ivory Tower Town! Isn't it an interesting name? To be honest, we don't really have any official name for our town.

The merchants who brought magical goods from the Ivory Tower to sell all over the world pitched tents here -- just to spend a few nights close to the tower. But more and more came and the tents ended up growing into the town you see.

So, we all came to call this area the Ivory Tower Town.
